About Me

Plant Breeder & Data Scientist

I am a Doctor in Genetics and Plant Breeding (PhD from UFV, 2025), currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at UFES, with a passion for solving complex agricultural problems through the convergence of quantitative genetics, field experimentation, and machine learning. My work focuses on making breeding faster, more precise, and resilient to environmental uncertainty.

With expertise in statistical modeling (BLUP, REML), genomic prediction, and simulation-based optimization, I develop tools and methods that help breeders make better decisions. I'm driven by open science and reproducible research practices.
